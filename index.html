Effective Cell-Division Degree Hours (ECDH)
Hourly heat accumulation weighted by cell-division response: base 43°F, optimum 72°F, disruption above 85°F, ceiling 91°F. 1.0 ECDH = one hour at optimal cell-division temperature. Window: DAFB 0 to 45 (Honeycrisp/Gala/Fuji/Pink Lady operational boundary).
How to read the bars
This year = cumulative ECDH since your logged bloom date through the latest hour we have weather for. Prior years show where each year's block was at the SAME DAFB as today for context. Avg = mean of available prior years at that same DAFB. Comparisons use each year's own bloom date so calendar offsets don't contaminate the comparison.
